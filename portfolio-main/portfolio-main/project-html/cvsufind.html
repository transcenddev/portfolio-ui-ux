Project Overview

CVSUfind is a web-based platform that I have developed to simplify the process of finding, claiming, and connecting lost and found items in Cavite State University. The platform provides a seamless experience for users, ensuring that lost items are effortlessly reunited with their rightful owners.

Problem

Losing personal items can be a frustrating and time-consuming experience. Traditional methods of reporting and searching for lost items often lack efficiency and can lead to a disconnect between the lost and the found. CVSUfind addresses this problem by creating a centralized platform that bridges the gap between the two, empowering users to easily navigate the recovery process.

Goal

The primary goal of CVSUfind is to facilitate a smooth and reliable lost and found experience through a meticulously designed UI/UX. By providing a user-friendly interface and streamlined tools, the platform aims to enable users to quickly and effectively report lost items, facilitate the connection between those who have lost and found items, and establish CVSUfind as a trusted and reliable solution for managing lost and found items.
