Attendance Page interface is designed to provide a streamlined and intuitive clock-in experience. The clear time and date display, along with the prominent "Clock in" button, create a user-friendly experience that can help improve attendance tracking and employee/visitor management for the Observerwards platform.

The addition of the "Clock out" functionality enhances the overall attendance tracking capabilities of the Observerwards platform, providing users with a seamless way to both clock in and clock out of their activities or events. This feature promotes better time management and attendance monitoring for the platform's users.
