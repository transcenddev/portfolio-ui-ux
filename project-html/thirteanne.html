Project Overview

Thirteean Milktea Shop is a local milk tea establishment in Naic, Cavite. The shop is looking to implement a user-friendly POS system to streamline their operations, improve customer experience, and better manage their business.

Problem

The current manual order-taking and cash management process at Thirteean Milktea Shop is inefficient, prone to errors, and creates delays in serving customers. The lack of a centralized system also makes it challenging to track sales, inventory, and customer data.

Goal

The primary goal is to design a POS system that will address the shop's pain points and provide a seamless experience for both the staff and customers. The system should enhance operational efficiency, improve order accuracy, and offer real-time insights to support business decision-making.

The login screen for the Thirteean Milktea Shop's POS system features a clean, minimalist design that aligns with the brand's visual identity. The intuitive user experience, with convenient features like "Remember Me" and "Forgot Password," is optimized based on user feedback to ensure a seamless and accessible login process across devices.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the business's performance, including sales, orders, and market trends, enabling the user to monitor and analyze the company's performance..

The Inventory Management Section demonstrates a strong focus on user-centricity, intuitive interaction, and efficient data management. The clean aesthetic, clear information hierarchy, and seamless integration of key features create a cohesive and empowering experience for business owners to effectively manage their product inventory.

The Orders interface offers a comprehensive and user-friendly order management system, presenting order details in a clear layout with flexible payment options and a streamlined workflow to efficiently process customer orders. The intuitive design and integrated features ensure an efficient and enjoyable experience for both the business and its customers.

The Orders section features a detailed order management interface that prominently displays the order recipient's name, ensuring transparency and personalization in the user experience. This attention to detail in highlighting the customer information reflects the overall thoughtfulness and user-centric approach applied throughout the application's design.

The Reports section features intuitive data visualizations that provide comprehensive insights into the business's top-performing products, empowering users with the information needed to make informed, data-driven decisions. The clear and concise presentation of sales volumes and inventory levels in a consistent bar graph format ensures the data is easily interpreted, making this module a valuable asset for tracking performance and driving strategic initiatives.

The Employees section presents a clean and organized user interface for managing the company's workforce. The intuitive layout with key employee details, such as name, contact information, and job roles, allows for efficient employee data management. This module demonstrates a user-centric approach to employee information, ensuring the business can effectively track and maintain its team.
